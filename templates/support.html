GGUS Helpdesk

You can raise a ticket via the GGUS helpdesk service. To use GGUS, you will need to sign up/log in with an EGI Check-in account, which GGUS will ask for before you can start creating your ticket.

Ticket Guidelines

Tickets are manually re-assigned to our support unit by the Ticket Process Managers (TPM) in GGUS. To ensure we that will receive a notification of your ticket, please:

  • Include a line in the ticket's description to request the re-assignment of the ticket to the PITHIA e-Science Centre support unit, which will help TPM with re-assigning the ticket to us later.
  • Set the ticket group to TPM.

If appropriate, also consider attaching screenshots and/or files to help us better understand your ticket.
