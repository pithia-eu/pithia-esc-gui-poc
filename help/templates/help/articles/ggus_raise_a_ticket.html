  1. Click the Raise a Ticket button to go to the GGUS helpdesk landing page.
  2. Click the "EGI Check-in" button to start the sign into the GGUS helpdesk service.
  3. Select the institution or login portal that you would like to sign in with. Logging in successfully will take you to the your GGUS user dashboard.
  4. Click the green "+" button in the bottom-left corner of the screen (if this is your first time raising a ticket, you can also click the "Create your first ticket button"). This will take you to the ticket form.
  5. Fill in the provided fields and click Create.

Ticket Guidelines

  • Use the provided text fields to give a description of your problem. We also recommend attaching screenshots and/or files to help us with getting a good understanding of your ticket later on.
  • Select the TPM group under the Group field. This assigns the ticket to the GGUS Ticket Processing Managers (TPM), who will re-assign the ticket to our support group later on.
  • Add a line to your ticket's main description, requesting it to be re-assigned to the PITHIA e-Science Centre support unit - this will help the TPM in re-assigning the ticket to us later.
